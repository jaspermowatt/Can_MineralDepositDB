Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) From Moore and Harris (2005): 1969-1976 After United Obalski Mining Company Ltd. defined several north and east trending radioactive areas in 1969, the company sold its claims to United Asbestos Corporation and Allied Mining Corporation. From 1969 to 1970, the two companies jointly carried out an exploration program including a fixed-wing airborne radiometric survey, line cut control grids, a ground scintillometer survey, and a 34-hole diamond drill program (1,558.3 metres). The companies compiled a very preliminary uranium ‘resource calculation’ for the combined West, West ext and East areas @ 13.3 million tons @ 0.38 lb/t U3O8 (190 ppm) 1976 -1985: The area was re-staked by Bomet Mines Ltd in 1976. Bomet then carried out a detailed and multi-staged exploration program including surface hand trenching and blasting, eight vertical diamond drill holes, bulldozer trenching of five areas (A to E) and ~ 45 shallow rotary air blast (‘RAB’) drill holes on their A & B Zones.
