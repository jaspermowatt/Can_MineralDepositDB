Qualified comments: (Applies to: regional geology) The Nemegosenda Lake Alkaline Complex consists of fine- to medium-grained syenitic to malingitic rocks which are bordered along the southern rim by an arcuate mass of coarse-grained nepheline syenite.

(Applies to: mineralization) The mineralization is hosted at the contact between syenite and red alkaline fenite. The alteration of the fenite to feldspar has involved the extraction of pyroxene and pyrochlore forming ions (Ontario Mineral Deposit Inventory).
