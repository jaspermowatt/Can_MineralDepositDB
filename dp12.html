Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) Development started in1954 and the mine opened in 1957 by Algom Uranium Mines Limited, which was incorporated into Rio Algom Mines Limited in 1959. The mine produced 2,360,239 short tons at about 0.09% U (based on resource estimates in 1960) between 1957 and 1960, and continued until 1964, when the mine waters were bled off to the Milliken Mine for uranium recovery (Jakubek et al., 1990). This full mining complex (underground mine, surface processing facilities and waste management area) was decommissioned during 1994-'95.
