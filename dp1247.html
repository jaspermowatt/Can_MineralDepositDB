Qualified comments: (Applies to: property geology) En-echelon sets of radioactive pegmatite dykes occur in a wide zone of easterly-dipping, folded gneisses along the southeast flank of the Cardiff pluton (Griffith, 1986). According to Griffith, garnet-sillimanite gneiss and scapolite gneisses host ore-bearing pegmatites, whereas pegmatites hosted by biotite paragneiss and syenite gneiss appear to be barren. Highly siliceous pegmatites and pegmatitic pyroxene granite are also preferentially associated with ore-grade radioactivity (Griffith, 1986).
