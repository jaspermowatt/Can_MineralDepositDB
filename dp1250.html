Qualified comments: (Applies to: location and access) The showing is located approximately 6.4 km W of the Fort Hall trading post and 280 km N-NW of Lynn Lake.

(Applies to: discovery and development) Denison Mines Limited took out Exploration Reservation 36 over the area between 1957 and 1971. Airborne and ground magnetic, electromagnetic and radiometric surveys were made in 1968. A uranium anomaly and a magnetic anomaly with a corresponding electromagnetic anomaly were found here. Two samples taken during a ground survey yielded trace and 0.33% U308; 0.01% and 0.06% ThO2 (Reservations 36-40, Non-confidential Assessment Files). The ground was reopened for staking in 1971.
