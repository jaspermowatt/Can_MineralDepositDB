Qualified comments: (Applies to: location and access) The showing is located 914 m E of the SE shore of Thanout Lake, approximately 274 km N-NW of Lynn Lake.

(Applies to: discovery and development) - Roman Corporation Limited took out Exploration Reservation 38 over the area between 1967 and 1969. In conjunction with Denison Mines Limited and Goldray Mines Limited airborne and ground magnetic, electromagnetic and radiometric surveys were undertaken as part of "Project SAM". A uranium anomaly was found at this location. It was trenched and sampled every 1.5 m. Assays from the trenching yielded only traces of U3O8 and Th02, but a chip sample assayed 0.06% U3O8 across 1.5 m (Reservations 36-40, Non-confidential Assessment File). - About 1.6 km S-SW of the trenching, a boulder assayed 0.19% U3O8, and 0.80 km S-SW of this boulder, a sample assayed 0.06% U303. - The area was reopened for staking in 1971.
