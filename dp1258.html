Qualified comments: (Applies to: location and access) The showings are located approximately 2½ miles W of Caddy Lake, 1½ miles S of Bear Lake, and in the immediate vicinity of Highway No. 44.

(Applies to: property geology) The showing occurs in a 60 foot wide zone of pegmatite and schist that extends for about 10 miles. This is the same zone of alternating bands of pegmatite and biotite schist in which U1 and U3 occur.

(Applies to: discovery and development) - In 1949 the showings were staked as Triangle No. 2 (W17592) and No. 6 (W17595) by Gregg Anderson; radioactivity was reported as 5-6 times background, and uraninite was identified. The property was worked and sampled intermittently until 1963. Details are found in the Manitoba Mineral Resources Library.
