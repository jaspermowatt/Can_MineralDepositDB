Qualified comments: (Applies to: location and access) Several anomalies are located on an island in northern Lac Brochet and along the N shore of Suffron Lake, approximately 214 km NW of Lynn Lake.

(Applies to: discovery and development) - Anomalies were covered by Exploration Reservation 65, belonging to Dynamic Products Ltd. between 1969 and 1971. Airborne and ground magnetic, electromagnetic and radiometric surveys were performed resulting in the discovery of these and several other uranium anomalies. These were followed by an on-rock survey using a BGS-1 scintillometer to measure cps (counts per second), with local results between 40 and 60 cps. More detail can be found in the Manitoba Minerals Resources Library. - The ground was reopened for staking in 1971.
