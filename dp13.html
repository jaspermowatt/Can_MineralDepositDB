Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) Milliken Lake Mines Limited commenced mining operation in 1958. The property was taken over by Rio Algom Mines Limited in 1958, and operated till 1964. It processed some 5.5 million tonnes of ore. The underground orebody extended to the adjacent Stanleigh mine, located about a kilometre to the north-northwest, and was mined by the Stanleigh operation that closed in 1996.
