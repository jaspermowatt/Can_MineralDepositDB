Qualified comments: (Applies to: mineralization) - The Achook Island RHY showing consists of copper and uranium mineralization within finely porphyritic rhyolite of the Cameron Bay Formation. The showing is approximately 50 m E of a steep, NE-striking fault between a coarse hematitic arenite to the W and red rhyolite to the E. The fault contains a stockwork of agate-like quartz. The rhyolite has been altered to a hematitic, quartz and mica assemblage with local chlorite and carbonate patches and contains quartz-hematite veinlets. - Two zones of mineralization were located, the best area containing 15 strongly radioactive occurrences in a 30 meter square area, and the second area 60 meters SW of the main zone, contained spectacular copper secondary minerals. A chip sample from the main zone assayed 0.11% uranium while the SW zone assayed 0.32% uranium and 1.56% copper across 3.3 meters. From high grade samples collected the following year, geochemical analysis revealed 1800 ppm uranium and 22000 ppm copper. Description taken from NORMIN.DB, June, 2007

(Applies to: discovery and development) - Work on this property began in 1949 when the GSC mapped the area at a regional scale. - The occurrences were jointly staked by PCE Exploration Ltd. and Mariner Mines in 1968. Blasting and trenching returned encouraging results of 2 to 3 per cent Cu. - In 1976, BP Minerals Ltd. staked most of Achook Island and began work on the property in 1977, when they preformed a reconnaissance survey over the property. In 1978, smaller grids were set up to cover the 'main' fault which they prospected with scintillometers and alphameters. Geological mapping, sampling and geochemistry were also performed on copper and uranium occurrences. The following 1979 field season the Rhy vein zone was trenched, sampled and mapped. The last bit of work was completed on the property by BP Minerals in 1982, when a scintillometer survey and detailed mapping were completed on the KIM claims. No further work on the showing has been reported. Description taken from NORMIN.DB, June, 2007

(Applies to: location and access) The RHY showing is in the centre of Achook Island.
