Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) Eldorado Mining and Refining Ltd. found a pitchblende occurrence by prospecting a cluster of airborne radiometric anomalies. Three trenches and a small pit were blasted. Samples from trenches were assayed. A trench 64 ft long varying from 3 to 8 ft in width exposes the main zone. Trench No. 2, located 220 ft west of Trench 1, is 20 ft long and varies from 5 to 13 ft in width. A small pit 5 ft long and 5 ft deep and Trench 3 lies 20 ft w of the pit.

(Applies to: rock assays) Four samples along the longer trench across the width of over 2 ft averaged 0.58 % U3O8 equivalent.

(Applies to: mineralization) Three showings within 200 m area occur on south side of the stream. Pitchblende in a narrow shear zone in biotite schist of Precambrian age. The shear zone strikes NE and joins a fault or fracture zone striking east. The mineralized zone traced for 60 ft. yellow secondary uranium minerals were observed. the mineralization was considered similar to the Beaverlodge type. pyrite, chalcopyrite and other sulphides were found at trenches.
