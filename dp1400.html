Qualified comments: (Applies to: rock assays) In 1956 COMINCO blasted trenches 4 to 7 and drilled 14 X-ray drill holes totalling 494 ft. Significant assays (>0.05 % U3O8) were from DH 1, 2 and 14 as 0.15 % over 0.4 ft, 0.15 % over 0.4 ft and 0.10 % U3O8 over 0.5 ft respectively. Trench 4 gave 0.11 % U3O8 across 2 ft, while trenches 5 to 7 assayed < 0.05 % U3O8 (Ref ID 5000013). In 1955 trenches 1, 2 and 3 were blasted. The assays were 0.1 % across 8 ft, 0.21 % across 5.5 ft and 0.1 % across 8.3 ft respectively.

(Applies to: discovery and development) U was found in 1954 by COMINCO prospectors. and geological mapping, trenching and 14 X-ray drill holes were drilled in 1956. Bragon and Island Groups were staked and explored in 1955-57.

(Applies to: mineralization) Pitchblende occurs with minor quartz, carbonate and hematite in fractures in gabbro, which is a younger intrusion in the Nonacho sediments, on claims 3 and 4 over an area 150 ft x 150 ft.
