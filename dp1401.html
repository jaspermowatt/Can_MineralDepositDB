Qualified comments: (Applies to: rock assays) Six trenches were blasted and the zone was exposed for 150 ft. Trench 3 gave best assays, 0.21 % U3O8 across 6.4 ft channel sampling, while the muck sample assay gave 0.45 % U3O8. Other trenches returned channel sampling assays ranging from 0.05 % to 0.14 % U3O8 across 2 to 3.2 ft.

(Applies to: mineralization) Pitchblende occurs as fracture-filling in gabbroic rock on claims 3 and 4. Gabbro, a younger intrusive in the Nonacho sediments, occurs as bands striking parallel to the Nonacho Group sediments - quartzites and conglomerates. The mineralized fracture zone trends 335 degrees and dips 60 degrees to the west. The zone is continuous for about 200 ft and then covered by 100 ft of overburden, beyond that the mineralization becomes sparse and patchy. Another small high grade showing is mentioned on the island, which occurs in granitized gneiss in a short fracture with pitchblende vein up to 1 inch wide.
