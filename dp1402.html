Qualified comments: (Applies to: reserve/resource assays) Drilling outlined a zone with estimated 27,000 tonnes of 0.17% U metal: Gandhi and Prasad, Ref ID 1515). Trapnell (Ref ID 1834) reports 20,000 tons of 0.2% U3O8.

(Applies to: mineralization) The main mineralized lens is 0.5 to 2 m thick, 150 m long and up to 60 m wide, dips 40-60 degrees to the east into the lake. The deposits rakes 40 degrees to the N-NE, subparallel to the plunge of the small fold approximately 75 m in amplitude. Pitchblende occurs in veins and disseminations and segregations close to veins, subparallel to the bedding in a 10 m thick unit of interbedded arkose and siltstone-shale. The pitchblende veins are up to 0.5 thick but usually much thinner veinlets and stringers.
