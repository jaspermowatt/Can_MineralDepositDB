Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) Zobo claims Group was staked in 1956 by Cominco. Two trenches and 8 drill holes were put in 1957-58. Try-me claims was held by Reward Uranium Mines Ltd in 1958 south of Cominco's Zobo Group and 1800 ft X-ray drilling done. The Ry 1-18 and LS 1-11 were restaking of parts of Atom and Zobo Groups acquired by Duke mining Ltd. in 1967. A detailed scintillometer survey in 1967 failed to discover any significant radioactivity other than the existing trenches. Uranerz in 1978 conducted ground radiometric surveys and assayed samples.

(Applies to: mineralization) On Zobo and Try-me Groups pitchblende and hematite veins are erratically distributed with quartz in north striking fractures in intrusive feldspar porphyry. Narrow Pitchblende veins < 2 cm wide and up to 15 m long occur in a breccia within highly metamorphosed Nonacho basal conglomerate adjacent to the Nonacho / basement contact.

(Applies to: rock assays) Total 1610 ft of diamond drilling was done in 8 holes to explore the radioactive zones discovered by trenching. The radioactive intersections were narrow 1/4 " to 2" ranging in 0.2 to 6.0 MR/hr up to 170 ft depth. Trench 1, 70 ft S-SW of Trench 2, discovered a lens 18 ft along strike assaying 0.25 % U3O8 across 2.7 ft. Trench 2 explored a zone for 70 ft along strike and averaged 0.60 % U3O8 across 5.0 ft.

(Applies to: rock assays) Uranerz sample 78029 assayed 2.00 % U3O8.
