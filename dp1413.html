Qualified comments: (Applies to: mineralization) The zone 1 is located along a NNW trending fault and associated breccia filling in a granodiorite - quartz diorite intrusion. The trend of mineralization zone changes from almost north to 076 degrees because of a drag fold. The higher radioactivity values occur mainly in the zone of brecciation. Previous sampling showed values of 0.05 to 36 % U3O8 equivalent. The zone is 100 ft long and varies 3 - 12 ft in width. Cu and Co stains are visible.

(Applies to: discovery and development) A.L Group of claims were staked in 1952 for Blue Grass Raymond Mines Ltd.. Three trenches were blasted on Zone 1. Zone 2 is 780 m NE of Zone 1.
