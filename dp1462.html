Qualified comments: (Applies to: mineralization) A number of sub-parallel quartz veins ranging in width from 1-6 ft. The granite- granite gneiss covers the south half of the claims, whereas Sosan Group sediments cover the northern part and the two are separated by a large E-W trending syenite dyke. On Claims MM-1 a radioactive fracture zone is traced to 60 ft in granodiorite. Later staking and assaying in 1951 by Radiore Uranium Mines Ltd. indicated that radioactivity was mainly due to Th.

(Applies to: discovery and development) Staked first by Frank Morrison in 1948-49, some pits were blasted and samples were assayed. In 1951 Radiore Uranium Mines Ltd restaked the property but initial assays were not duplicated.
