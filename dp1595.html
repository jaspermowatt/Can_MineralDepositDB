Qualified comments: (Applies to: mineralization) The showings occur in the dolomite/limestone breccias within five metres of the contact with the diorite. Massive Co-Ni mineralization appears to be confined to the breccia zone, in areas not replaced with magnetite. Often only minor veinlets are found between breccia clasts, but elsewhere matrix fillings of 30%-60% coarse dolomite or grey Co-Ni arsenide are found. Description from NORMIN.DB, June 21, 2007.
