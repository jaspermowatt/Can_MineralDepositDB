(Applies to: discovery and development) - The Larry group of claims along the eastern shore of Watch Lake were reportedly first staked by H. Gonzales to cover an uranium occurrence. It was staked and optioned several times, and subjected to trenching, blasting, and mapping before the claims were cancelled in 1971. - P. Dunlop staked C.B. 7190 in 1975. Mid-North Uranium Limited controlled the property as of 1976 (Mid-North; Corp. File).
