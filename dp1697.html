(Applies to: miscellaneous comments) This showing is listed in original sources as a vein showing and could be a fracture filling in the Archean Marjorie Hills assemblage. It is near the mapped surface contact of the Martell syenite (see next comment from the original uranium-thorium database). It is also relatively close to the unconformably overlying Kunwack Formation and a bit further from an intrusive Nueltin Granite. All are considered potential favourable influences for uranium enrichment.
