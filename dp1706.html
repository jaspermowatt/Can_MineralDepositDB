Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) Cominco Limited, in joint venture with E & B Explorations Limited , carried out follow-up work in 1979 on an airborne radiometric survey over the Kent Peninsula area done during 1978. The surface work included geological mapping and ground check of radiometric anomalies. No significant discovery was reported except for a small occurrence at Kongyuar Point. Here one rock cliff has three unconformities as reported by Campbell (1978), who mapped the regional stratigraphy of the Bathurst Inlet-Melville Sound area. The basement is Archean, overlain by early Mesoproterozoic fault-controlled Tinney Cove Formation, which is fanglomeratic. It is unconformably overlain by middle Mesoproterozoic Ellice Formation, which in turn is unconformably overlain by younger Mesoproterozoic strata. After the surface exploration reported by Wilton (1980), no further uranium exploration seems to have been done on the Kongyuar Point showing or in the region around it.
