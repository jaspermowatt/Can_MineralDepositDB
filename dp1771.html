Qualified comments: (Applies to: diamond drill hole descriptions) To test the extension of the mineralized zone intersected in drill hole TK13-87, 3 drill holes were drilled in 1985. Drillhole TK13-92 intersected pitchblende vein hosted by mylonitized diorite above the ultramafic intrusive. The radiometric peak on the vein is 28,000 cps. DH TK13-90 intersected small mineralization with maximum peak of 4,400 cps. The mineralization in TK13-90, TK13-92 and TK13-91 may be the extension of the zone intersected in DH TK13-87.

(Applies to: mineralization) Uranium mineralization zone is in mylonite above the hanging-wall of an ultramafic intrusive, which cuts the mylonite zone. The mineralization is associated with discontinuous veinlets of hematite and/or calcite. The mineralization was deemed to be too weak and not too encouraging, therefore PNC recommended no further work.
