Qualified comments: (Applies to: location and access) The FXO claims cover an island on DeVries Lake.

(Applies to: structure) Uranium-bearing mineralization is hosted in feldspar porphyry intrusion-related tension fractures and siliceous pockets, or in small amphibolite pockets in extrusive feldspar porphyry. Regional faulting appears to have no relation to uranium mineralization on either the FXO or UGI claims. Comment from NORMIN.DB, July 19, 2007.

(Applies to: mineralization) Magnetite-rich hydrothermal veins showing pitchblende, uraninite and minor secondary uranium-bearing oxide mineralization are hosted in intrusion-related tension fractures. Radioactive mineralization is also concentrated in small siliceous pockets in the groundmass of quartz feldspar porphyry (FXO claims) and amphibolite pods (UGI claims). High-grade samples from the FXO claims returned assays of 4.65% U3O8 (grab), and 10.5% U3O8 and 11.8% U3O8 (trench). description from NORMIN.DB, July 19, 2007.
