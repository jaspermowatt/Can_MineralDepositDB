Qualified comments: (Applies to: mineralization) A small isolated patch in a quartz vein cutting granitoid basement rocks assayed 0.46% U3O8 + 0.94 oz/ton Ag + 0.15% Cu + 0.10% Pb. The wall rock adjacent to the quartz vein contains isolated radioactive patches associated with malachite and azurite staining. A sample collected from the altered wall rock assayed 0.17% U3O8 + 0.3 oz/ton Ag + 0.32% Cu + 0.03% Pb + 0.01% Zn + 0.05% Ni. The mineralized area is 2.5 to 3 metres wide and does not appear to have any significant strike length. The showing is situated on the face of a cliff so it was not possible to trace it.
