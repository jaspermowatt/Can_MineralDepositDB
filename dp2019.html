Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) The area of S prospect was intensively explored by Cogema-SDBJ joint venture during 1980 to 1984. Surface work included airborne radiometric and magnetic surveys, lake and stream sediment surveys, and ground work on gridded area including prospecting, soil survey, magnetic and VLF surveys. Trenching, short holes with Winkie drill and long holes with wire-line drill. Total number of holes drilled is 60. Uranium mineralization is in thin carbonate veins and stringers in gabbro and contain a few percent sulphides. They are accompanied by silicification, chloritization and bleaching of gabbro. The best uranium assay is 0.233 % U in a selected sample of gabbro. in addition to uranium anomalous values of Au, Cu, Pb, Zn, Ag and they have been reported in several samples. The property was restaked by Boreale Exploration Inc. in 1999 for its gold content. Resampling old Cogema drill core and of the trenches. Best results obtained are 0.5 g/t over 1.09 min hole OELS-302 at 3 m below Trench 3 Au (Girard, 1999), and selected samples of thin carbonate veins of the S showing gave values up to 13.63 g/t Au (Milord, 2000).
