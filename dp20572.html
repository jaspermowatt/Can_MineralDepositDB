Qualified comments: (Applies to: mineralization) Radioactivity is associated with red sandstone clasts of the Paleoproterozoic polymictic conglomerate (unit P2). Only a small percentage of clasts are radioactive. The clasts range from 5 to 50 cm in size and are scattered over a wide area. There is no indication that the mineralization occurs in the matrix of the conglomerate. The 9 samples were assayed and the results ranged from < 0.001 to 1.1 % U3O8. The high assay values coincide with anomalies of >5000 cps.
