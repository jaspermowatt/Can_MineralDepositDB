Qualified comments: (Applies to: mineralization) All detected anomalies were apparently due to red sandstone clasts within the two polymictic conglomerate units. This unit is found about 100 meters up section from the unconformity. The clasts are scattered over a wide area and the distances of several hundred metres may separate the anomaly points. The clasts are 5 - 50 cm in size. There is no indication of mineralization in the matrix.

(Applies to: rock assays) One sample with scintillometer reading of 1250 cps returned values of 0.604 % ThO2 and 0.032 % U3O8 - a very Th:U ratio. Since the mineralization is widely scattered and restricted to red sandstone clasts it is not considered very significant.
