Qualified comments: (Applies to: diamond drill hole descriptions) Of the 14 drillholes 5 intersected significant radiometric peaks with highest 9,700 cps. However the width of the peaks are narrow, and the mineralized zones do not correlate between holes or from holes to the surface.

(Applies to: mineralization) Most of the anomalies encountered in drillholes are associated with veins of hematite, chlorite, &/or calcite, &/or pitchblende while one hole pyrite, chalcopyrite and bornite were found. The chloritization is pervasive throughout the mylonite, while hematization is associated occasionally.
