Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) The uranium mineralization, discovered in 1959, is located on the NW side of Quetachou Bay, within a magnetic and garnet bearing pegmatite dyke trending 030° with a southeasterly dip at 70° associated with the Turgeon Lake Intrusive Complex.
