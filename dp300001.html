Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) Ground scintillometer prospecting discovered U showings by Shell Minerals and worked by C.E.G.B. in early 1980s. In 2005 Commander Resources acquired 40,000 hectares of land in southern Newfoundland covering a strike length of more than 100 km and also acquired an additional 1600 claims covering the Hermitage Flexure geological belt. An agreement with Pathfinder Resources increased another 1429 claims to the east and west of the Commander Resources ' claims. In fall of 2005 Commander Resources did mapping, 16 composite chip sampling, prospecting and 14 back hoe trenching.

(Applies to: rock analysis and sample assays) Sixteen composite rock chip samples assayed 0.13 to 1.32 % U3O8, while six of those assayed > 0.67 % U3O8.

(Applies to: discovery and development) A 5500 line km airborne radiometric, magnetic and geophysical survey was done in mid 2006 by Aeroquest International Ltd. for Commander Resources. Most of the known uranium showings - Blue Hills, Main, Muise, Chan, Hawksnest, White Bear River prospects and Doucette have clear radiometric and geophysical signature. The airborne geophysical survey has extended the magnetic anomaly to the E and W of Hook Lake for a total length of over 1.5 km. Detailed ground prospecting was carried out following the survey.

(Applies to: mineralization) Main showing is in radioactive boulders stretching south from Hook Lake and along east side of the Wayne's Pond for about 165 m, but the scattered boulder fan covers an area 500 x 450 m. U in bedrock occurs 1070 m ENE of the showing as stratabound mineralization at the contact of volcanic tuff and biotite sandstone-quartzite of Bay du Nord Group of Ordovician age approximately 400 m east of the White Bear - 3 showing (DEPNO-300018).
