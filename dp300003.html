Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) Radex Minerals optioned Nalco IV in 1969, prompted by high radon values in Fluorspar mines in Burin peninsula and found veins of pitchblende with barite, galena and sphalerite near Little Lawn Harbour. They performed water, soil geochemistry and geophysical surveys from 1969 to 1973. Geological Survey of Canada did airborne radiometric survey of Burin peninsula at 5 km spacing in 1974 and province-wide reconnaissance lake sediment survey in 1976. Bp Minerals Ltd. acquired claims in 1977 and discovered U in narrow shear zones within quartz-rhyolite porphyry. They performed mapping, ground scint survey, and soil and stream surveys.

(Applies to: mineralization) Anomalous radioactivity found in rock as well as in soils.
