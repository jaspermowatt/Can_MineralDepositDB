Qualified comments: (Applies to: diamond drill hole assays) Five shallow drill holes drilled in 2006. The best value was 0.084 over 0.5 m from 10-10.5 m in hole 06-01. Several other uranium values from holes 06-01, 06-02 and 06-05 ran 0.053% over 0.3metres, 0.03% U3O8 over 0.5 metres and 0.023% U3O8 over 0.8 metres. These intervals occurred within wider zones of anomalous uranium mineralization (Commander Resources Press Release Feb. 02, 2007).

(Applies to: miscellaneous comments) A new uranium showing discovered by Commander Resources is centred on airborne radiometric anomaly.

(Applies to: mineralization) Scattered anomalous outcrops extend for a strike length of over 400 m

(Applies to: rock analysis and sample assays) An altered outcrop area just north of the ST-129 was highly anomalous over 65 m. The samples from this area included five that ran over 0.20% U3O8 with two assaying 0.80% and 0.31% U3O8. Two other samples assayed between 0.10% and 0.19% U3O8 and a further seven were highly anomalous containing between 0.045% to 0.083% U3O8. Samples were taken from the central, high count radiometric zone which is up to 35 metres wide within the 65 metre wide alteration zone.
