Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) These showings were first noted by Scott and Conn 1950 (Ref ID# 7000014) and described by Falconbridge Nickel Mines Ltd (Barry 1980, Ref ID # 7000049). Noranda staked the area in 1985. In 2005 Commander Resources acquired claims and performed geochemical, geophysical surveys, and back-hoe trenching for 158 m in14 trenches and composite sampling. Commander Resources continues prospecting in this area in 2006 and 2007.

(Applies to: mineralization) Quartz veined staurolite porphyroblastic semipelitic schist hosts uranium mineralization. The semipelite is interbedded with volcanic tuffs. The mineralization is related to the leucocratic aplite dykes and veins that are thought to be related to the Devonian granitic intrusions.
