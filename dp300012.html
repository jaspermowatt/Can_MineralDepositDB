Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) Shell Canada Ltd. staked the area in 1980 and carried out geological and geochemical work. B.P. Selco in 1985 and CEGB Exploration worked in 1985 and 1987 respectively. The area is acquired by Commander Resources in 2005.

(Applies to: genetic model) In 1980s the uranium exploration model was based on peribatholithic type of uranium deposit (syngenetic-hydrothermal type). However subsequent exploration and discovery of several uranium showings indicated a stratabound U concentration in Ordovician Bay du Nord Group interbedded metasediments and metavolcanic tuffs (epigenetic- volcanic- related). In the later model the U is of volcanogenic origin and got remobilized by the heat of the Peter Snout Granite intrusion into shears and veins.
