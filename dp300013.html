Qualified comments: (Applies to: mineralization) Galena, sphalerite, barite, fluorspar vein contains pitchblende and locally abundant pyrite and marcasite and minor chalcopyrite in a matrix of pink carbonate in a shear zone at the contact between a Carboniferous porphyry and Cambrian black hornfels.

(Applies to: discovery and development) Radex Minerals Ltd. and joint venture with NALCO explored for uranium in 1969 and then extended the exploration for base metals. From 1970 to 1972 Radex performed geochemical prospecting, geological surveys, sampling, geophysical surveys, trenching and shallow drilling. The first U showing of the province was found here. The surveys outlined favourable geochemical anomalies of Mo, Sn and base metals, high grade Pb-Zn-U veins, high grade quartz-pegmatite-Sn veins and high grade and disseminated Mo (Cu-U) in float and/or bedrock. From 1975 to 77 Newfoundland Department of Mines and Energy conducted a geological and geochemical survey of the Marystown and St. Lawrence areas. During 1978-79 BP Minerals carried out exploration on NALCO Lot IV.
