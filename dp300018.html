Qualified comments: (Applies to: rock analysis and sample assays) Six 2-3 kg rock chip samples of felsic volcanic boulders assayed from 0.09% to 0.56% U3O8 and another 3 grab samples assayed 0.512, 0.754 and 0.564 % U3O8 (Commander Resources Press releases, Ref ID-7000065 and 7000066).

(Applies to: discovery and development) The showing is known since 1950 and was examined for Falconbridge Nickel Mines Ltd. in 1980 by Barry J.M. (Ref Id- 7000017). Noranda staked it 1985. The property was acquired by Commander Resources Ltd. in February 2005 as White Bear 114 claims. They performed geochemical, geophysical surveys and back-hoe trenching and sampling. The prospecting continues in 2006 and -07.

(Applies to: mineralization) Main radioactive zone consists of about 10 sq m of angular boulders in a boulder field covering more than 100 sq m. The mineralization is hosted by fine to medium grained non-welded quartz phyric tuff where porous, coarser and interbedded with pelitic metasediments. The U mineralization is parallel to the primary banding in meta-tuff. Locally U was remobilized in overlying pelitic rocks by subsequent deformation and metamorphism. The felsic metavolcanics exhibit silica and sericite alteration.
