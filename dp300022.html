Qualified comments: (Applies to: location and access) The point location of the symbol represents the approximate centre of the broad radiometric anomaly or several anomalies which cover an area 1600 m in E-W and 475 m in N-S direction. One major high anomalies occurs approximately 400 m to the west and the other 700 m to the east.

(Applies to: rock analysis and sample assays) Four rock chip samples assays ranged from 1.00 to 7.09 lb/tonne U3O8.

(Applies to: mineralization) U mineralization is stratabound along the contact between felsic volcanic rocks and graphitic sediments of Ordovician Bay du Nord Group. The mineralized crystal tuff contains pyrite in a cherty matrix.

(Applies to: discovery and development) In 1980-81 Shell Canada did geological and geochemical work in this area. B.P. Selco staked it in 1985 and C.E.G.B. Exploration worked in 1987. Commander Resources acquired claims in 2005 and continue the exploration and prospecting for U in 2007.
