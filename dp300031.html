Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) In 1950s Claybar Uranium and Oil Ltd. drilled for oil and encountered gas. Westfield Minerals Ltd. explored in mid 1970s for base metals with discouraging results but discovered many radiometric anomalies that started the uranium exploration in the Deer Lake Basin. Hudbay Uranium Ltd. in 1977 carried out lake sediment geochemistry, airborne radiometric and ground geological and geophysical surveys in the Deer Lake Basin and discovered many radiometric anomalies. In 2001 Altius Minerals acquired the property and carried out lithogeochemistry, geophysics and 96-site overburden drilling with radiometric surveys and extensive till and heavy mineral analyses to outline the source of the radiometric boulders to be from north. In December 2005 Altius Minerals completed an extensive drilling program in the favourable source area and found anomalous radioactivity and alteration in several holes (Altius Resources New Release 14 June 2006 Ref ID=7000072).
