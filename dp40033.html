Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) A study of the Claude mine adit in 1979 led to exploration along zone between Earl River and Peter River gneisses, and to discovery of Dominique-Peter deposit in 1980 and of Dominique-Janine deposit in 1984. The Dominique-Janine North open pit was mined during 1989-1991. Dominique-Janine South open pit operated during 1994-1997. Dominique-Janine South Extension underground mine operated from 1995 to 2002. Dominique-Janine South (or D-J Extension or DJX) Open Pit Production: 1195 t U from 366057 t of ore; Underground Production: 1388 t U from 445348 t ore; miscellaneous pods: 4.617 t U from 604 t ore. In 1987, a gold circuit was added to the mill. This circuit recovered a total of 8000 oz of gold and 800 oz of silver.

(Applies to: production) Two mines: Dominique-Janine South, 100 m from Cluff Lake shore, produced 950000 t of ore averaging 5.8 % U; Dominique-Janine North, located 1100 m to its north, produced 230000 t averaging 3.8 % U.
