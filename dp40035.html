Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) The Collette Zone was located in 1993 by drilling of the Saskatoon Lake conductor 1.5 km NW of the Main or Anne zone of the Shea Creek area. It is close in character and size to the Anne zone.

(Applies to: mineralization) Mineralization at Shea Creek occurs in bends in the traces of a conductive graphitic unit and in fault zones discordant to the northwest trend of local lithologies. Displacements appear to strike north-northeast (clockwise of the lithological trends). Zone(s) of disseminated, nodular and massive mineralization proximal to the unconformity is recognized in the Collette showing.
