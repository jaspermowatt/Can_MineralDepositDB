Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) This occurrence represents the last numbered one of the 12 occurrences examined in 1976 by Western Mines Limited (Schindler, 1977; Knox, 1980). It is located on the west side of Showing Lake about 2.5 km west of the well explored showing 76-1 (Westmin) on the north side of the lake. A sample of the uraniferous siltstone float (cobble) close to the nose of an anticline contains 1.18 lb U3O8/short ton (0.05 % U) determined by chemical assay (Schildler, 1977, p. 26). The float is regarded as locally derived, which implies that the three uraniferous horizons of Showing 76-1 about 2.5 km to the east likely extend in the area of Showing 76-12.
