Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) Accumulations of moderately radioactive feldspathic quartzite were discovered in i983, and were more detailed work on grid 1.7x3.0 km grid located more boulders including higher grade ones, that formed a well defined a dense train in direction 310 to 330°, and the a pebble orientation study established the latest glacial movement in direction 325°. Geological mapping defined a series of bands of siltstone and feldspathic quartzite trending 045° along the regional trend of the Amer fold belt in the area. Two localities of strong bedrock mineralization feldspathic quartzite about 500 m apart are on the shore of a lake at the east corner of the grid and are 350 m up-ice of the main boulder train. The mineralized unit is characterized higher magnetite content, and magnetic survey corresponding two parallel anomalies. The survey also revealed a strike-slip fault trending 165°. Additional work included petrochemical study of 89 selected radioactive boulders and outcrops, which were group into 6 lithological varieties.
