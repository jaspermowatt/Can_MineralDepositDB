Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) The Tabb A and B showings are among a group of showings discovered in the Bear Valley during 1978 as a result of combined airborne and ground radiometric surveys conducted by the BP Minerals Canada Ltd. and Union Carbide Exploration Ltd. Joint Venture. Geological mapping and geochemical surveys in the region were carried out in the same year. Diamond drilling in 1979 included 3 holes at the Tabb B showing and one hole at Tabb A showing, which is 200 m south of the B showing. In 1982 Anaconda Canada Exploration Ltd. carried out additional geochemical surveys, and in 1983 drilled 8 more holes totalling 625 m. Five of the holes were at Tabb B showing, one was at Tabb A showing, and 2 others in their vicinity. Results of the drilling was however disappointing. Exploration was resumed in 2004 by UNOR Inc. in joint venture with Adriana Resources Inc. (UNAD JV). During 2005-'06 the Tabb showings were examined. Assays on 12 surface samples ranged from 0.06 to 4.0 % U.
