Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) The Far prospect was discovered at the same time as the Rah prospect in 1976 during a follow-up of Cominco's 1975 regional lake water geochemical survey. As a result it the RAH 1-103 and FAR 1-110 claims were staked in 1976. exploration work in 1977 included detailed mapping, prospecting, trenching, airborne and ground radiometric surveys, and radon survey. A weakly radioactive anomalous zone in FAR 54 claim was explored further by 2 trenches and 2 pits in 1977. The trenches excavated in paleoregolith at the base of Hornby Bay Group and the pits were 200 m to the east on outcrop of altered flaggy sandstone of Hornby Bay Group.
