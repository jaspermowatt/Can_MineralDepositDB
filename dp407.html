Qualified comments: (Applies to: location and access) This occurrence is 2 km NW of Burnt Lake, 5 km S of McLean Lake, and 19 km ENE of the Michelin deposit.

(Applies to: mineralization) Uranium mineralization is disseminated within the Aillik (former Upper Aillik) Group felsic pyroclastic rocks. Good mineralization is reported to be associated with mafic lapilli, and with strong hematization, late brecciation, and high strain zones. A high U/Th ratio suggests that the uranium has been remobilized after its original deposition and reconcentrated where conditions are favourable.
