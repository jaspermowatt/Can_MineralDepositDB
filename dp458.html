Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) From Moore and Harris (2005): 1968-1978: The area was explored by Canadian Johns Manville Company Ltd. The company’s ten-year exploration efforts included several geophysical surveys (air and ground based), regional and local scale prospecting and geological mapping, extensive line-cut control grids, grid and regional-scale soil/silt sampling, bulldozer assisted surface stripping and trenching, drill holes (packsack, RC and diamond) and preliminary uranium ‘resource calculations’ on the No.1, No.1 Ext, H5, H52 Zones. Kish’s 1975 report (DP-310) is first to identify the Mont Laurier area biotite paragneisses as a potential host of uranium mineralization.
