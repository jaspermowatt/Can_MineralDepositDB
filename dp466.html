Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) Discovered during a radiometric survey in 1971, after an extensive horizon of quartz pebble conglomerate with a uranium-pyrite matrix. Canico calculated a non-43-101 compliant rough estimate of grade and tonnage of 9,365,000 tons grading 0.054% U3O8 or 1.08 lb/ton for a total of 10.1 million pounds (Worsfold, 1974). Virginia Mines investigated the possibility of Au on the property in 1998. After visiting the property in August, 2007, Strateco Resources signed an agreement with Virginia to acquire 100% of the property in consideration of company shares. The Apple property now consists of 194 claims covering 9,928 hectares recorded in the name of Strateco Resources Inc. It is currently undergoing target-testing activities (Cook and Chamois, 2008).

(Applies to: miscellaneous comments) This Apple paleoplacer unconformably overlies Archean rocks, including Mesoarchean crust. It is constrained by geochronology and stratigraphic plus cross-cutting relationships to be Neoarchean in age. The uranium values are correlated with pyrite content in the conglomerate. Proterozoic metasedimentary rocks of the Sakami Formation (< 2.07 Ga) exposed to the northeast also show anomalous radioactivity.
