Qualified comments: (Applies to: host rocks) Deposit group in ferruginous metatuffs interbedded with basalt and shales (Valhalla), brecciated siltstones with interbedded basic volcanics (Skal) and lens of altered greywacke.

(Applies to: alteration) Uranium-vanadium mineralisation is confined to zones of red-coloured hematite- magnetite-carbonate alteration within the brecciated shales and tuffaceous sediments (Eggers, 1999). Sodic metasomatism and albitites are ubiquitous. Higher-grade uranium mineralisation is coincident with the presence of sodic pyroxenes (aegerine) and sodic amphiboles (arfvedsonite). The brecciation may be associated with the activity of the Mount Isa Fault zone, the trace of which projects through the area.
