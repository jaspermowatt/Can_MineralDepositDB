Qualified comments: (Applies to: related magmatic rocks) The mineralization is associated with sheets of medium to fine grained leucogranite and pegmatite with granophyric, equigranular, porphyritic and pegmatitic textures. Compositions are not restricted to alaskite, and the term sheeted leucogranite is preferred. There are up to six phases (A-F) of leucogranite is the area, of which the fourth (D) and fifth (E) phases are uraniferous and have intruded the Damaran sequence late-tectonically (Nex, Kinnaird, and Oliver, 2001).

(Applies to: diamond drill hole assays) Recent drilling by Bannerman Resources has extended the strike of the most promising Anomaly A from 1.7 kilometres to 2.3 kilometres. Several promising intersections have been reported, and it was newly discovered that mineralization is not confined to the late leucogranites, but occurs in the host metasediments as well.
