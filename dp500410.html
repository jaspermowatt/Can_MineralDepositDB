Qualified comments: (Applies to: mineralization) More than 35 uranium prospects, or separate zones, are known at Bokan Mountain. According to the US Bureau of Mines, they may contain uranium, REE, beryllium, tantalum, niobium, zirconium, and thorium. One of the zones, the Ross Adams Mine, produced 1.3 million pounds of U3O8 at an average grade of 0.76% from 1958 to 1971 from an open pit. It is reported to be pipe-like in shape.

(Applies to: miscellaneous comments) This property is now (2007) in the hands of Ucore Uranium Inc. and Landmark Minerals Inc,; these companies have embarked on aggressive exploration, including geophysics and drilling.
