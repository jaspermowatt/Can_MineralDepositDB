Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) Canadian Johns-Manville discovered uranium mineralization in 1967. In the past, the area was owned by Allied Mining, Canadian Johns-Manville, Mont-Laurier Uranium Mines, and is now owned by Ressources Strateco (2010). Hanson West corresponds to a large radiometry anomaly over 800m by 300m that was stripped in the 1970s, with two drilled in 1973. From January to March 2007, Ressources Strateco drilled 28 holes (2274.6 m)

(Applies to: mineralization) Radioactive pegmatites associated with pink granites contains more Th than U. Neodymium-rich phases crystallize adjacent to allanite and in rims on uraninite.
