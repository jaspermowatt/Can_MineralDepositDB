Qualified comments: (Applies to: discovery and development) Historical exploration by Denison Mines Limited in the 1960s and early 1970s outlined anomalous uranium mineralization in the Double S occurrence. In the 1980s and 1990, the Quebec government conducted geochemical surveys, which confirmed that the Turgeon Lake Intrusive Complex contained anomalous uranium mineralization. In 2006, Uracan Resources Ltd. conducted follow up work. Field work related to surface exploration and surface samples was conducted between June and August 2006, as well as between May and late September 2007. A limited geological mapping program was conducted between September 27th and October 6 h, 2007. In additional to the field program, an airborne geophysics program was conducted from July to September 2006, and also from April through July 2007.
