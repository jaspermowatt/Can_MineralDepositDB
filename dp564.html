Qualified comments: (Applies to: diamond drill hole assays) 1,226 m of drilling in 6 holes was completed by Uracan in 2008 to follow up drill results from winter 2007. Multiple mineralized intervals were noted with up to 95.2 m of 0.016% U3O8 encountered in LP-08-04, 52-5m of 0.01% in LP-08-01, and 19.5 m of 0.013% noted in LP-08-05. (Applies to: discovery and development) The uranium mineralization, discovered in 1967, is located 2.9 kilometres south and 1 kilometre east of the eastern extremity of Turgeon Lake, within a magnetic granite and pegmatitic granite of the Turgeon Lake Intrusive Complex.
