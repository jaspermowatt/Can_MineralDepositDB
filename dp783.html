Qualified comments: (Applies to: mineralization) Mineralization is associated with breccias related to tension fractures within multiple shear zones. The best grades occur where shears merge and the wall rock is either granitic or diopsidic. Pyrite is widely disseminated through the breccias, and is accompanied by chalcopyrite locally replaced by sooty chalcocite, and by local galena. Pitchblende and gummite are finely disseminated in breccia fragments and matrix. Thucolite was noted locally. Hematization is pervasive around ore zones.
