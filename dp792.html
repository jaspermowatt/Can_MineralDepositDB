Qualified comments: (Applies to: property geology) The occurrence area exposes the basal conglomerate of the Martin Formation unconformable overlying a south-plunging syncline in basement quartzite of the Tazin Formation. Numerous en echelon fractures occur above and below the unconformity.

(Applies to: mineralization) Mineralization consists of pitchblende that accompanies carbonate and hematite in numerous en echelon fractures in the Tazin quartzite and overlying Martin conglomerate. In most places the fractures are too widely spaced to form ore shoots, except at the Lake Shore Zone where two small orebodies occur underground, near the unconformity.
