Qualified comments: (Applies to: property geology) The property is situated along a N40 W striking and 40 SW dipping fault, the ABC Fault, that separates unmetamorphosed Martin Group siltstone and arkose in its hanging wall from Murmac Bay metasedimentary rocks (meta-argillite, meta-quartzite, chlorite-epidote schist) and granite in its footwall. The main showing occurs in the footwall along a steeply dipping sheared contact between metasedimentary rocks and granite. Mineralization consists of hematitic fracture fillings with subordinate pitchblende, pyrite, chalcopyrite, galena, quartz, chlorite, and calcite.
