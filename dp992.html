Qualified comments: (Applies to: mineralization) This occurrence consists of a 1.6 kilometre long boulder train trending approximately NNE. The boulders largely consist of white pegmatite cutting locally graphitic and calcareous gneisses and contain uranium stain and locally uranium-copper mineralization localized near the pegmatite metasediment contacts. The boulders overlie outcrops of similar Wollaston gneisses cut by pegmatite dykes oriented counterclockwise (030/87) to the NE-striking (045/70) foliation. Outcrops too show uranium staining.
